Platform features

Everything you need to excel

A focused platform for serious steno students — no clutter, just the tools that matter.

Real-time Error Analysis
Every transcription is instantly evaluated — full mistakes, half mistakes, omissions, extra words, capitalisation and full stop errors all counted separately.
Audio Dictation Tests
Weekly tests with real dictation audio. Listen on your phone, transcribe on your PC — the platform handles both seamlessly.
Progress Tracking
Track your error percentage, WPM and pass rate over time. See exactly where you're improving and what needs more work.
Monthly Leaderboard
See how you rank against your batchmates each month. Healthy competition drives consistent practice.
Passage Library
KC passages, TN DOTE dictations, Pitman basic lessons and general passages — all in one organised library with speed filters.
Instructor Correction
Auto-evaluation gives instant results. Your instructor can review and override any score — human judgment combined with automation.
Simple workflow

From homework to result in minutes

1
Get your homework
Your instructor sends a passage and audio link via WhatsApp. Practice the shorthand at home.
2
Listen & transcribe
Open the platform, find the passage, listen to audio on your phone, and type your transcription.
3
Instant evaluation
Submit and get an instant annotated result — every mistake highlighted and counted by type.
4
Instructor review
Your instructor reviews the result, corrects if needed, and tracks your progress over time.
