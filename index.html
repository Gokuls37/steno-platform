Platform features

Everything you need to excel

A focused platform for serious steno students — no clutter, just tools that matter.

Real-time Error Analysis
Full, half, omissions, extra words, capitalisation and full stop errors — each counted separately with colour-coded highlights.
Audio Dictation Tests
Weekly surprise tests with real audio. Listen on your phone, type on your PC — both work seamlessly together.
Progress Tracking
Track your error %, WPM and pass rate over time. See exactly where you're improving month by month.
Monthly Leaderboard
See how you rank against your batchmates every month. Healthy competition drives consistent practice.
Passage Library
KC, TN DOTE, Pitman and general passages — all organised by category and speed in one library.
Instructor Correction
Auto-evaluation gives instant results. Your instructor can review and override any score — human judgement plus automation.
Simple workflow

From homework to result in minutes

1
Get your homework
Your instructor sends a passage + audio link via WhatsApp. Practice the shorthand at home.
2
Listen & transcribe
Open the platform, enter the test code, play audio on your phone, type your transcription on PC.
3
Instant evaluation
Submit and get an instant annotated result — every mistake highlighted and counted by type.
4
Instructor review
Your instructor reviews the result, corrects if needed, and tracks your progress over time.
